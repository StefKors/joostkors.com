New Hospital at Gaustad

Oslo / University hospital expansion / 120.000 m² / RATIO arkitekter

The further development of the Gaustad area with the goal of creating one, integrated, functional and modern hospital where national, regional and local functions are gathered. The chosen concept ëcombí continues the structure and functional principles of the existing Rikshospital, but places the wards on top of the treatment areas. This insures a compact hospital with short distances and effective treatment of patients.

Our process was focussed on developing as many different solutions as possible, with an emphasis on functional and economical effectiveness. As a member of the design team my tasks were mainly to find and explore alternatives in volume, function and structure. As the project developed my responsibilities shifted to focus more on logistics and technical aspects of the hospital.
